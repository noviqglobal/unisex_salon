Good To Know

Frequently asked questions.

Everything first-time guests usually ask, answered before you even have to.

We’re ListeningAsk Us Anything
Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Walk-ins are welcome, but appointments get priority and shorter wait times — especially on weekends. Booking via WhatsApp or our contact form takes under a minute.

What hygiene protocols do you follow?

Every tool is sanitised between clients, all linen is single-use per guest, and our studio follows ISO-aligned hygiene checklists reviewed monthly.

Do you offer services for both men and women?

Yes — Style Up is a unisex studio. Our hair, skin, spa and grooming menus are designed for every guest, with dedicated specialists across both.

How does the membership program work?

Pick a tier — Silver, Gold or Platinum — and you'll get a flat discount on every visit plus loyalty points redeemable against future services, valid for 12 months from sign-up.

What's your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 4 hours' notice to reschedule or cancel, so we can offer your slot to another guest. No-shows may be asked for a small advance on the next booking.

Do you offer bridal or at-location services?

Yes — our bridal team travels for wedding-day styling within Pune. Trials are always done in-studio first to lock in the final look.

Where exactly are you located, and is parking available?

We're a two-minute walk from Wipro Circle in Hinjewadi Phase 1, easy to find from anywhere along the IT corridor. On-site parking is available for both two- and four-wheelers.
